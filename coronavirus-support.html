Keeping You Safe

Coronavirus Help & Support

The health and safety of our clients and carers has always been our top priority. Here's how we continue to manage infection control across every visit.

Our infection prevention and control practices are built into everyday care, in line with CQC guidance and current public health advice. If you have specific concerns about a vulnerable client or family member, please get in touch and we'll talk it through.

🧼

Rigorous Hygiene Standards

Handwashing, sanitising and PPE use are standard practice for every carer on every visit.

🩺

Health Monitoring

Staff are trained to recognise symptoms early and follow clear guidance if unwell.

📋

Risk Assessments

Individual client risk assessments are kept up to date and reviewed regularly.

📞

Open Communication

We keep families informed and are always reachable with questions or concerns.

ℹ️

Have a specific question? Our care team is happy to talk through our current infection control measures for your situation — just give us a call or send a message.
