Training & Quality Assurance

Every Carer, Fully Trained
and Fully Vetted

Our commitment to quality doesn't stop at recruitment. Every member of our team receives continuous, structured training so your care is always in safe, capable hands.

Staff Training In-House Training Nurses Revalidation
🎓
01 — Staff Training

Recruited for Compassion, Trained to a Standard

Every carer we employ is recruited first for compassion, then developed through structured, accredited training before they ever step into a client's home. All staff are DBS checked and inducted against CQC-regulated standards.

  • Enhanced DBS checks for all staff
  • Moving & handling certification
  • Medication administration training
  • Safeguarding adults training
  • First aid & basic life support
  • Infection prevention & control
🏢
02 — In-House Training

Ongoing, Practical Development

Beyond initial induction, our team runs regular in-house training sessions to keep skills current and responsive to the specific needs of the clients we support — from dementia awareness to person-centred care planning.

  • Dementia awareness workshops
  • Person-centred care planning
  • Communication & de-escalation skills
  • Nutrition & hydration best practice
  • Record keeping & documentation
  • Refresher training every year
⚕️
03 — Nurses Revalidation

Supporting Our Registered Nurses

As a nurse-founded organisation, we understand the importance of NMC revalidation. We support our registered nursing staff through the revalidation process — including reflective practice, CPD tracking and practice-related feedback — so clinical standards stay current across the team.

  • CPD hours tracking & guidance
  • Reflective accounts support
  • Practice-related feedback collection
  • NMC Code alignment
