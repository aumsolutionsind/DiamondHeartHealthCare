Our Story

Founded on Real Hospital Experience

Diamond Heart Health Care Limited was founded by a nursing professional with over 25 years of experience working in hospitals. That first-hand clinical background shapes everything we do — from how we assess a new client's needs to how we train our carers.

What began as a mission to bring genuinely compassionate, clinically-informed support into people's homes has grown into a trusted home care provider across Bolton, Greater Manchester, Manchester, Salford and Wakefield — without ever losing sight of the person-centred approach it was built on.

Our Vision

Independence, With Dignity

Our vision is to provide the highest-quality home care for adults and older people, enabling them to live as independently as possible within their own homes and communities — supported, not replaced, by the people who care for them.

  • Care that adapts to the person, not the other way around
  • Supporting independence rather than creating dependence
  • Setting the standard for quality home care in the North of England

Our Mission

Supporting Those Who Need Us Most

Our mission is to help those who cannot retain their independence due to illness or injury. We are committed to delivering compassionate, professional care that restores confidence, dignity and quality of life — one person at a time.

  • Rapid response when families need urgent support
  • Personalised care plans reviewed and updated as needs evolve
  • Close coordination with NHS and social care where required
Our Values

Not Just Words on a Page

At Diamond Heart Health Care, our core values are the guiding principles behind every visit, every care plan and every conversation with a client or family.

  • Integrity — doing the right thing, even when no one is watching
  • Compassion — treating every client the way we'd want our own family treated
  • Respect — honouring each person's preferences, routines and choices
  • Excellence — continuously training and improving as a CQC-regulated provider
